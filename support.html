Common Issues

  • Alarms not firing - Disable battery optimization for Noor ul Haya and allow exact alarms in Android Settings.
  • Wrong prayer times - Verify city, calculation method, and madhab for Asr in Location settings.
  • Qibla inaccurate - Calibrate your compass by moving your phone in a figure-8 pattern away from metal objects.
  • Location denied - Set your city manually in Settings → Location.
