FAQ

Questions before you download

Is Noor ul Haya free?

Yes — completely free with no in-app purchases or advertisements. Download the APK from GitHub or Google Play when available.

Does the app collect my personal data?

No accounts are required. Location is used only on-device for prayer times. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Why are my prayer times different from my local mosque?

Mosques may use different calculation methods or manual adjustments. Try changing the calculation method under Settings → Location to match your region.

Prayer alarms are not working — what should I do?

Allow notifications and exact alarms, disable battery optimization for Noor ul Haya, and ensure alarms are toggled on for each prayer in the dashboard.

Can I use the app without GPS?

Absolutely. Turn off auto-detect and manually select your city and calculation method in Settings.

How do I delete my data?

Clear app data in Android Settings or uninstall the app. Instructions are on our Delete Data page.
