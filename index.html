Everything You Need

Built for the complete Muslim lifestyle

Seven thoughtfully designed modules working together to keep your worship organized, on time, and meaningful.

Prayer Times

Accurate Fajr through Isha times with Hijri calendar, live countdown to the next prayer, and daily progress tracking.

  • Adhan Engine
  • Hijri Date
  • Tracker

Smart Alarms

Reliable prayer notifications that wake you for Fajr and remind you throughout the day — never miss a salah again.

  • Exact Alarms
  • Per-Prayer
  • Permissions

Qibla Compass

Real-time compass with live heading, Qibla bearing calculation, and turn-by-turn alignment guidance.

  • Live Compass
  • GPS Based
  • Alignment

Quran

Read and reflect with a clean, distraction-free Quran module designed for daily recitation and study.

  • Surahs
  • Clean UI
  • Offline

Duas & Supplications

Curated collection of authentic duas for every moment — morning, evening, travel, and daily remembrance.

  • Arabic
  • Translation
  • Categories

Beautiful electronic prayer beads counter for dhikr — tap to count, reset anytime, track your daily remembrance.

  • Tap Counter
  • Haptic
  • Reset
